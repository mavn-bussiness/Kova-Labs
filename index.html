Global tools weren't built for this market.

Offline-first architecture

Everything works without internet and syncs when connected. Power outages and slow connections won't interrupt your business.

Compliance built by country

URA, NSSF, PAYE for Uganda. KRA for Kenya. FIRS for Nigeria. No manual configuration — it's baked in from day one.

Mobile money as a first-class citizen

MTN MoMo and Airtel Money aren't afterthought plugins. They're core to how Kova handles payments and reconciliation.
