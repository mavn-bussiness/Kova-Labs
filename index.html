The Origin Story

Born from a real
hospital problem.

“During my ICT internship at a government hospital, patients had no idea which queue they were in or which window to go to. Staff printed paper notices and taped them to walls. There was no patient tracking, no digital signage, no NFC for staff IDs — just paper. I knew software could fix this.”

That experience became the foundation of Kova Labs. ScreenIQ, KovaCard, StaffDesk — every product solves a problem I watched African organisations struggle with daily. This isn't a solution looking for a problem. This is a real problem with software-sized solutions.
