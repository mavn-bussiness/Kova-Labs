Core Features

Everything a fleet manager
in Africa actually needs.

Built around the real challenges of managing vehicles across Uganda's roads — patchy internet, fuel theft, untrustworthy trip logs.

Live Map Tracking

All vehicles plotted on a real-time map with 30-second refresh. Works on 2G, 3G, 4G. Offline data syncs automatically when the device reconnects.

Geofence Alerts

Draw zones on the map. Get instant SMS or in-app alerts when any vehicle enters or exits a restricted area — site, school, city boundary.

Fuel & Mileage Reports

Automatic trip logs, distance reports, and fuel consumption analytics. Catch over-fuelling and ghost trips instantly. Export to PDF or Excel.

Driver Behaviour Scoring

Score each driver on speeding, harsh braking, and idling. Identify high-risk drivers before accidents happen. Reduce insurance and maintenance costs.

Any GPS Hardware

Works with Concox, Teltonika, Coban, and any MQTT/GPRS-compatible tracker. No proprietary hardware lock-in — use what you already have.

Multi-User & Multi-Fleet

Managers, dispatchers, and drivers each get role-specific views. Manage multiple vehicle categories — trucks, saloons, motorcycles — in one account.
