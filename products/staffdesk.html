Core Features

Everything your HR team
actually needs.

Automated Payroll

Run payroll in one click. Gross-to-net calculations with NSSF (10% employee + 10% employer) and PAYE automatically computed per URA brackets.

URA & NSSF Compliance

Auto-generate PAYE returns, NSSF contribution reports, and WHT statements. File directly or export in URA-ready formats. Never miss a deadline.

Leave Management

Annual leave, sick leave, maternity/paternity leave — all tracked automatically. Employees apply on mobile; managers approve with one tap.

Digital Contracts

Generate and store employment contracts digitally. Version-controlled, role-specific templates for Uganda's different employment categories.

Employee Self-Service

Staff access payslips, apply for leave, and view contracts on their phones. Works on 2G. Reduces HR admin load by 60%.

KovaCard Integration

Sync with KovaCard NFC tap-in/out data for automatic attendance. No manual timesheets — payroll is calculated from real clock-in records.
