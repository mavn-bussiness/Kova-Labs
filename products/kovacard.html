The Problem We Saw

Paper registers at the gate.
Every single day.

"At the hospital, visitors signed a paper book at the gate. Staff showed laminated ID cards that could easily be duplicated. Nobody knew who was in the building at any given time. Patient records were accessed with zero authentication."

KovaCard solves this with affordable NFC smart cards and RFID readers — connected to a cloud dashboard that logs every tap, every entry, every identity check in real time.

Core Capabilities

One card. Every door.
Total control.

Smart Staff ID Cards

NFC cards with embedded employee data. Tap to clock in/out, access restricted areas, and authenticate digitally — replacing manual registers and paper IDs.

Access Control

Grant and revoke access to buildings, wards, server rooms, and offices from one dashboard. Time-limited access for contractors and visitors. Real-time audit logs.

Patient Wristbands (RFID)

RFID wristbands for hospital inpatients. Scan to instantly pull up patient records, medication schedules, and ward assignments. Zero paper, zero errors.

Attendance & Payroll Sync

Tap-in/out data feeds directly into StaffDesk payroll. No manual attendance sheets. Overtime, absences, and late arrivals are calculated automatically.

Cashless Payments

Load credit onto NFC cards for cafeteria payments, printing charges, or internal services — without handling cash. Works offline, syncs when connected.

Visitor Management

Issue temporary NFC visitor badges at reception. Track who's in your building at all times. Automatically invalidate passes at the end of a visit.
